Privacy Policy

Workphony ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Workphony is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

1.2 Local Data Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Timer settings and preferences
  • Favorite sound selections
  • App configuration settings

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to use. The app functions entirely offline and processes all data locally on your device.

3. Permissions We Request

Workphony requests the following permissions to provide its core functionality:

3.1 Notifications Permission

  • Purpose: To send timer completion alerts and Pomodoro session notifications
  • Data: Only notification content (timer status, session completion)
  • Storage: Processed locally, not stored or transmitted

3.2 Vibration Permission

  • Purpose: To provide haptic feedback for timer alerts
  • Data: No data collected, only device vibration triggered

3.3 Keep Device Awake Permission

  • Purpose: To prevent screen sleep during active Pomodoro sessions
  • Data: No data collected, only system-level screen management

4. Data Security

Since Workphony operates entirely offline and does not collect personal information, your data security is maintained through:

  • Local-only data storage on your device
  • No network transmission of personal data
  • No cloud storage or external servers
  • Standard Android/iOS security measures

5. Third-Party Services

Workphony does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app is completely self-contained and operates independently.

6. Children's Privacy

Workphony does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages and does not require any personal information to function.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the policy on our GitHub repository
  • Including the updated policy in future app releases

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear all app data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Revoke permissions through your device settings

9. International Users

Workphony is designed to work offline and does not transfer data across international borders. All data processing occurs locally on your device.
