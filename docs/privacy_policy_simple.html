Workphony Privacy Policy

Workphony is committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Information We Collect

None. Workphony does not collect any personal information. The app works entirely offline and stores only your preferences locally on your device.

Local Data Storage

The app stores only these items locally on your device:

  • Timer settings and preferences
  • Favorite sound selections
  • App configuration settings

Permissions

Workphony requests these permissions:

  • Notifications: For timer completion alerts
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback
  • Keep Device Awake: During active Pomodoro sessions

Data Security

Your data is secure because:

  • No personal data is collected
  • All data stays on your device
  • No network transmission
  • No cloud storage
